Teacher Education Resource Room

The Teacher Education Resource room supports faculty, teacher education students and graduates from our BK and FACS programs. The materials are provided to serve as supplemental aids, and can be used in the room and/or taken out for a two-hour period. All materials must be signed out with the student's name and contact number. Materials available are books, DVDs, games, an empathy belly, Ready 4 Life Family and Consumer Sciences program, home interior/architect materials, and much more. The Teacher Education Resource Room is located in Rivers 260.
