Family Therapy Clinic - Our Services

Listed below are some of the services we offer:
  • Individual therapy: provided from a systemic perspective
  • Couple/marriage therapy: addresses growth and strength areas of the couple
  • Family therapy: works to help family achieve a higher level of functioning
  • Premarital services: focus on the couple's growth and strength areas
  • Medical family therapy: assists clients in gaining strength during an illness or disability while working collaboratively with medical professionals in the community
  • Cotherapy teams: teams of two therapists working with families and clients
  • Reflecting teams: team discussion of client's strengths halfway through the session while family members observe their discussion
  • Videorecording opportunities: help clients and therapists review their work (with client's permission)
