Family Therapy Clinic - Appointments

Appointments are made by individuals interested in receiving therapy services. Concerned family members, and/or community professionals (e.g., physicians, school administrators, teachers, counselors, ministers, attorneys, mental health professionals, social services, law enforcement agencies, judges, and others) are invited to participate in the therapy process upon receiving the client's consent. Referrals are not needed.

The best times to call are between 8:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday. Voice-mail messages left during the weekdays will be returned within 24 business hours. Weekend calls (Friday afternoon through Sunday evening) will be returned on the first working day of the following week. All calls are confidential.

Therapeutic teams, where live supervision is provided, meet approximately two to three times a week typically in the evenings. Clients must consent to work with a therapeutic team.

If you are interested in making an appointment, please call (252) 737-1415.
