Human Development and Family Science

Welcome to the Department of Human Development and Family Science (HDFS). If you are interested in learning about and working with children and families or becoming a therapist, then we have a program for you! Career opportunities in Human Development and Family Science are as varied as our undergraduate programs, which include Birth through Kindergarten Education, Family and Consumer Sciences Education, Child Life, and Family and Community Services and our graduate programs of Child Development and Family Relations, Marriage and Family Therapy, and Medical Family Therapy. Learn more about these exciting and growing career opportunities!
