Family Therapy Clinic - Our Therapists

The ECU Family Therapy Clinic is staffed by six licensed AAMFT-approved supervisors and approximately 20 therapists-in-training. Clients may request the therapy team, cotherapy, or one-on-one therapy session format when they schedule an appointment. If a client needs special considerations for his/her therapist, we will do our best to accommodate the client's needs to the extent to which this is feasible.
